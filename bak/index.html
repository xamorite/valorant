JETT

Representing her home country of South Korea, Jett's agile and evasive fighting style lets her take risks no one else can. She runs circles around every skirmish, cutting enemies before they even know what hit them.

SPECIAL ABILITIES

C

CLOUDBURST

INSTANTLY throw a projectile that expands into a brief vision-blocking cloud on impact with a surface. HOLD the ability key to curve the cloud.

Q

UPDRAFT

INSTANTLY impel Jett high into the air.

E

TAILWIND

INSTANTLY impel Jett in the direction she is moving. If Jett is standing still, she will impel forward.

X

BLADE STORM

EQUIP a set of highly accurate throwing knives that recharge on killing an opponent. FIRE to throw a single knife.

BIOGRAPHY

From the streets of Seoul to the battlefields of VALORANT, Jett is the ultimate speedster. Her agility isn't just for show; it's a weapon that keeps her one step ahead of the competition.
